About VoyageVista

VoyageVista is a student project designed to help people discover famous destinations and easily plan their dream trips.

Our Goal

This website was developed by our group as a requirement for Coursework 2. We selected a topic we are truly interested in and built a complete, responsive website that runs smoothly on localhost.

The project helped us improve our skills in web design, development, and teamwork. We hope you enjoy exploring it as much as we enjoyed creating it!

Thank you for visiting!
